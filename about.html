Who We Are


THE TAMIL NADU SAFETY PROFESSIONALS WELFARE ASSOCIATION (TN-SPWA) is a non-profit organization, Founded in 2020. That enables collaboration, knowledge sharing, career enrichment, and skills development across all safety professionals, improve their art and skills for the benefits of their career development and for the wider benefit of employers in various disciplines of industries.

We provide education, advocacy, standards development and a professional community to our members in order to advance their careers and to en-courage the effective exchange of information and experiences between members of the TN-SPWA.

    Objective

  • To unite for mutual benefit those safety professionals engaged in and responsible for safety through their services.
  • To serve as a central agency for the gathering and exchange of technical information in the field of safety.
  • To serve as the voice of safety professionals.
  • To actively promote the highest standard of safety professionalism.
  • To educate the public on safety that will lead to a safer environment.
  • Social Awareness on Safety.
